# Architecture Freeze

The architecture has been approved.

From Sprint 2 Phase 2.2 onwards:

- No new entities may be added.
- No entities may be removed.
- No relationships may be changed.
- No naming conventions may be modified.
- No architectural patterns may change.

If implementation reveals an architectural issue:

1. Stop implementation.
2. Document the issue.
3. Propose an ADR.
4. Wait for approval.

Only after approval may the architecture be modified.
